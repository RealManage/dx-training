# Description: Generate monthly board meeting agenda
# Arguments: meeting_date

Generate the monthly HOA board meeting agenda for $ARGUMENTS.

## Template Structure:

### HOA Board Meeting Agenda
**Date:** $ARGUMENTS
**Location:** Community Clubhouse / Virtual (Zoom)
**Called to Order:** [President Name]

#### I. Call to Order (2 min)
- President calls the meeting to order

#### II. Roll Call & Quorum Verification (3 min)
- Secretary takes roll call
- Minimum 3 board members required for quorum
- Note: Meeting cannot proceed without quorum

#### III. Approval of Previous Meeting Minutes (5 min)
- Motion to approve previous minutes
- Second required
- Vote: Voice vote or roll call

#### IV. Financial Report (10 min)
- Treasurer presents:
  - Outstanding dues summary
  - Late fee collections this month
  - Budget vs actual YTD
  - Reserve fund status
- Questions from board members

#### V. Violation Review (20 min)
Query violations requiring board action (90+ days old):

For each violation, present:
- Property address
- Violation type and description
- Days since reported
- Current escalation level
- Total fines accrued
- Staff recommendation

Board actions available:
- Approve fine increase
- Grant extension
- Initiate legal proceedings
- Dismiss violation

#### VI. New Business (15 min)
- Pending items from last meeting
- New resident architectural requests
- Community maintenance issues
- Upcoming events

#### VII. Homeowner Forum (10 min)
- Open floor for homeowner comments
- 3-minute limit per speaker
- Board may respond or take under advisement

#### VIII. Adjournment
- Announce next meeting date (first Tuesday of next month)
- Motion to adjourn
- Second required
- Meeting adjourned at [TIME]
