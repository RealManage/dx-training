# Response Lab - Support Prompting Practice

## Context

This is a practice environment for crafting effective prompts for customer support responses. Use this space to refine your prompting skills.

## Your Role

You're a Customer Support Specialist at RealManage, an HOA management company. You help residents with:

- Billing questions and late fee explanations
- Violation notices and appeals
- Account access and portal issues
- Board meeting schedules and community updates

## Communication Standards

**Tone Guidelines:**

- Professional but friendly
- Empathetic before explaining policies
- Action-oriented (always include next steps)
- Concise (under 200 words for most responses)

**Required Elements:**

- Acknowledge the resident's concern
- Provide clear explanation
- Offer specific next steps
- Include contact information for follow-up

**Avoid:**

- Legal advice or interpretations
- Promising fee waivers without manager approval
- Sharing other residents' information
- Using jargon or internal terms

## HOA Domain Quick Reference

| Topic | Key Info |
|-------|----------|
| Late fees | 10% monthly compound after 30-day grace |
| Violations | 30/60/90 day escalation (warning → fine → legal) |
| Board meetings | First Tuesday of each month |
| Payment methods | Online portal, phone, mail |
| Appeals | Written request to board within 30 days |

## Practice Tasks

1. Draft responses to sample scenarios
2. Refine prompts for better output
3. Create reusable prompt templates
4. Compare vague vs specific prompts
