RealManage Late Fee Schedule
============================

Grace Period: 30 days
Interest Rate: 10% monthly (compound)
Maximum Cap: 3x original amount (per state regulations)

Escalation Timeline:
- 0-30 days: No fee (grace period)
- 31-60 days: 10% of principal
- 61-90 days: 21% of principal (compound)
- 91+ days: Continues compounding monthly

Formula: Total = Principal * (1.10 ^ months_overdue)

Payment Plan Options:
- 3 months: No additional fee
- 6 months: 5% administrative fee
- 12 months: 10% administrative fee

Contact: collections@realmanage.com
