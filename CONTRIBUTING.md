# Contributing

This repository is maintained by the RealManage Developer Experience (DX) Team. Development happens internally and changes are mirrored here for public access.

## Using This Material

You are free to:

- **Fork** this repository and adapt the courses for your own organization
- **Share** the material with appropriate attribution to RealManage
- **Build upon** the content under the terms of the [CC BY-SA 4.0 License](./LICENSE)

## Reporting Issues

If you find errors, broken links, or have suggestions:

- Open a discussion on your fork
- Email the DX Team at dx-team@realmanage.com

## Internal Contributors

RealManage team members: all contributions flow through our internal GitLab instance via merge requests. See the team wiki for workflow details.
